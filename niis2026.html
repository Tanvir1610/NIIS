About NIIS 2026

International Conference on Next-Gen Integrated and Intelligent Systems

Welcome to NIIS 2026

The International Conference on Next-Gen Integrated and Intelligent Systems (NIIS 2026) is a premier platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to converge and share their innovative ideas and research findings.

Our conference focuses on cutting-edge domains including Electronics, VLSI, Embedded Systems, AI/ML, IoT, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Smart Networks, aligning with national missions like Semicon India, Digital India, and Atmanirbhar Bharat.

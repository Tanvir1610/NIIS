Submission Guidelines

NIIS 2026 International Conference

Complete guidelines for paper submission, review process, and publication requirements

Submission Overview

Authors are invited to submit the Full Length Research/Review Papers. The papers will be peer-reviewed and only the accepted and registered papers will be considered for presentation during the conference and publication in the proceedings.

⚠️

Important Guidelines

  • The organizers of NIIS2026 regard plagiarism as serious professional misconduct
  • All submissions will be screened for plagiarism and when identified, the submissions will be rejected
  • The NIIS 2026 doesn't encourage withdrawals after the paper is accepted
  • At least one of the authors of an accepted paper needs to register for the conference and to present the paper(s), through an Online Mode presentation
📚

Paper Publication Guidelines

  • Conference Organizers are discussing with possible publishers for SCOPUS index publication (with additional publication charge)
  • All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings with ISBN/ISSN number
  • Papers must be original and not published elsewhere
  • High-quality research contributions are expected
📝

Submission Requirements

  • Submit full-length original research papers only
  • Papers must NOT be submitted/published/under consideration elsewhere
  • Electronic format: Doc or Docx only
  • Submit through CMT Conference Management System only
  • English is the official language of the conference
🔍

Review Process

  • All papers undergo rigorous peer-review process
  • Acceptance is strictly based on reviewers' comments
  • Double-blind review process ensures fairness
  • Authors will receive detailed feedback
  • Review timeline: 4-6 weeks from submission
🎤

Presentation Guidelines

  • Online mode presentation required
  • At least one author must register and present
  • Presentation time: 15-20 minutes including Q&A
  • Technical requirements will be provided upon acceptance
  • Presentation slides must be submitted in advance

🚨 Plagiarism Policy

The organizers of NIIS2026 regard plagiarism as serious professional misconduct. All submissions will be screened for plagiarism and when identified, the submissions will be rejected immediately. Authors are expected to maintain the highest standards of academic integrity.

📋 Registration Requirement

At least one of the authors of an accepted paper needs to register for the conference and to present the paper(s) through an Online Mode presentation. Papers without registered authors will not be included in the proceedings.

🏆 Publication Opportunity

Conference Organizers are discussing with possible publishers for SCOPUS index publication (with additional publication charge). All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings with ISBN/ISSN number, ensuring wide visibility for your research.

Important Notes

📌 Final Reminder

Acceptance of a full-length submission is strictly based on the reviewers' comments. Authors are encouraged to follow all guidelines carefully and ensure their submissions meet the highest academic standards. For any queries regarding the submission process, please contact the conference organizers.
